# TezFlow / NSCNS Logistics Intelligence Platform
## 3-Minute Demo Video Script & Walkthrough

This document provides a step-by-step guide to recording a seamless, high-impact 3-minute demonstration video of the supply chain platform. 

### 🎬 Pre-Recording Setup
1. **Open Three Browser Windows/Tabs:** 
   - Tab 1: `hq.html` (National Command)
   - Tab 2: `warehouse.html` (Warehouse Logs)
   - Tab 3: `driver.html` (Driver Portal)
2. **Window Arrangement:** For the best visual impact, either record a wide screen with all three windows side-by-side, OR record a single screen and smoothly switch between the tabs.
3. **Reset State:** Refresh all three pages to ensure a clean start. Make sure the dark mode/bento UI is active.

---

### ⏱️ Section 1: The Platform Overview (0:00 - 0:45)
**Goal:** Hook the viewer with the premium "Earthbound Tactical" design and high-level analytics.

* **[HQ Tab]** Start on `hq.html`. 
* Briefly scroll to show the massive Bento Grid UI, the Live OpenStreetMap, and the predictive widgets (Explainable AI, Crisis Prediction, Dynamic Allocation).
* Point out the **Chaos Score** widget in the top left, which should be in a "Standard" or "Watch Zone" state.
* **[Warehouse Tab]** Switch to `warehouse.html`. Briefly show the active inventory levels and the 3D shipment logs. 
* **[Driver Tab]** Switch to `driver.html`. Show the live tracking of the truck on its standard route (the green line from Agra to Amritsar).

---

### ⏱️ Section 2: Triggering the Crisis (0:45 - 1:30)
**Goal:** Show the AI reacting to sudden, catastrophic real-world events.

* **[HQ Tab]** Go back to `hq.html`.
* Click the **"🌊 Demo"** button in the top right navigation bar.
* **Do not click anything else.** Let the system take over. 
* Point out the **Flood Demo Console** that appears in the bottom right.
* **Step 1 & 2:** Watch the Red Flood Polygon appear over Punjab. Note how the **Chaos Score spikes** into the red zone and the system triggers an emergency response.
* Switch back to the **[Driver Tab]** just as the system calculates the bypass.

---

### ⏱️ Section 3: The AI Auto-Reroute (1:30 - 2:15)
**Goal:** Demonstrate the real-time, cross-platform synchronization and the self-healing routing engine.

* **[Driver Tab]** You are now viewing the driver's screen.
* Watch as the map *instantly* drops the straight path and recalculates the **Rajasthan Bypass** (swooping far west through Jaipur and Bikaner to avoid the red flood zone).
* Show that a warning notification has popped up for the driver, alerting them of the HQ Auto-Reroute.
* **[Warehouse Tab]** Quickly switch to `warehouse.html` to show that the Warehouse also received the emergency "Rerouted" event in their system logs.

---

### ⏱️ Section 4: Resolution & AI Allocation (2:15 - 3:00)
**Goal:** Show how the system stabilizes itself after a crisis.

* **[HQ Tab]** Return to the National Command center.
* The Demo Console will be finishing **Steps 4, 5, and 6**.
* Point out the **Dynamic Allocation** widget and map animations showing resources (Food, Medicine, Fuel) being redirected from safe zones (Delhi, Jaipur, Bhopal) into the affected regions.
* Watch the final **Step 7**. The system will stabilize, and the Chaos Score will drop back down to safe levels.
* Conclude the video by showing that despite a massive disaster, the supply chain survived without manual human intervention.

---

### 💡 Tips for a Great Recording
- **Don't rush:** Let the map animations finish drawing before switching tabs.
- **Mouse Movement:** Move your mouse smoothly to point at key metrics (like the Chaos Score or the Red Polygon) so the viewer knows where to look.
- **Narrate the "Why":** If you are doing voiceover, don't just say *what* is happening, say *why*. (e.g., "The system detected a flood, so our multi-node AI immediately calculated a massive bypass through Rajasthan to keep medical supplies moving.")
